Monitoring and Evaluating the Effectiveness of the plan/ Treatment Fidelity

Ongoing training and monitoring of the RBT will occur on a weekly basis to ensure proper implementation of the behavior program. The BCBA/lead analyst will conduct weekly competency checks to ensure proper program implementation. The BCBA/lead analyst will graph the data, depicting the results of the competency checks on monthly progress reports. Also, the BCBA/lead analyst will collect IOA data at least once a month to ensure proper data collection.

Objectives included in assistant competency checks:

  1. Data collection
  2. Interventions/Environmental manipulations.
  3. Replacement goals used to teach the proper alternative to the maladaptive behavior targeted.
  4. Use of reinforcement
