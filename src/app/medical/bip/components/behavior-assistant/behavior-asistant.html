Behavior Assistant Rationale

Specific duties and responsibilities: Collect data on target behaviors identified for reduction/acquisition/replacement, implement the behavior program and train the caregivers on data collection and implementation of the procedures outline within the plan. Work Schedule and Schedule of activities: Behavior assistant will work with consumer Monday-Saturday, times vary. During that time all programs will be implemented. Ongoing Assessment Responsibilities: Behavior Assistant will be responsible for collection of ongoing ABC data that will be used to assess the continued efficacy of the program/ interventions. Behavior Assistant will also record data sheets provided and attached to the program Training Procedures for Caregivers: Following competency based training by the behavior analyst of the caregivers with regards to the interventions outline within the behavior program, the behavior assistant will complete competency checks of the caregivers’ ability to collect data, implement replacement programs and follow through on the interventions. Competency checks will include demonstration/modeling of procedures, observation of caregiver implementing replacement/acquisition programs, as well as corrective feedback when warranted.
